# Third-Party Licenses & Attribution — CrustaVirus DB

CrustaVirus DB integrates data from numerous public biological databases. This
document records the terms of use for each source and the attribution statements
required by their licenses.

---

## 1. NCBI (National Center for Biotechnology Information)

**Sources used:** Nucleotide, Taxonomy, Protein, SRA, GEO, PubMed  
**URLs:**
- https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/nuccore/
- https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/taxonomy/
- https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/protein/
- https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sra/
- https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/
- https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/

**Terms:** NCBI databases and data are in the public domain in the United States
and are freely available for reuse, provided that NCBI is cited as the source.
See https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/home/about/policies/.

**Required attribution:**
> Data sourced from NCBI databases (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) are in the
> public domain and made available by the National Center for Biotechnology
> Information, U.S. National Library of Medicine.
