About AquaVir-KB

A comprehensive knowledgebase for aquatic invertebrate viruses, curated for research integrity and reproducibility.

📦 Data Availability Statement NAR Compliant

All raw genomic data are deposited under NCBI GenBank / INSDC accessions referenced in each entry. Sequence-derived annotations, curated host-pathogen links, and evidence records are released as structured TSV and JSON dumps under CC-BY 4.0 via the Data Download portal. Phylogenetic trees (Newick + SVG) and protein 3D-structure metadata are bundled in the same release. No reasonable request for data will be refused; for bulk data not covered by the portal, contact data@aquavir-kb.org.
