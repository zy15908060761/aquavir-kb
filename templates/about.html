ℹ️ About AquaVir-KB

Aquatic Invertebrate Virus Knowledge Base

Project Overview

AquaVir-KB is the world's first comprehensive knowledge base dedicated to aquatic invertebrate viruses. The database integrates multi-dimensional data including virus taxonomy, host associations, literature evidence, protein functional annotations, geographic distribution, and phylogenetic analysis. It covers eight host phyla: Arthropoda, Mollusca, Cnidaria, Echinodermata, Porifera, Annelida, Platyhelminthes, and Rotifera, and includes over 1,600 virus species and more than 17,800 isolates.

Formerly CrustaVirus DB v1 (crustacean virus database), expanded in 2026 to cover all aquatic invertebrate viruses.

Sustainability

AquaVir-KB is committed to long-term operation. The database will be maintained at this URL for at least 5 years following publication (through 2031). Data is archived with Zenodo (DOI: [ZENODO_DOI]) ensuring permanent availability. The source code is available on GitHub.

Data updates are integrated from NCBI GenBank, ICTV, and literature mining on a quarterly basis. All curation workflows are documented and reproducible.
